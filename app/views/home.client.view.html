Frequently asked questions



How long does it take to get a report?

When you order a vehicle report, Grumpy Cars’ systems immediately access databases to compile the report. This usually takes less than 3 minutes, however it can take up to 30 minutes. Your report will be emailed to the email address you provided during payment as soon as it is ready.

Where can I find the VIN?

The Vehicle Identification Number (VIN) can be found on the vehicle itself, either on the dashboard, or on one of the door posts (where the door latches when it is closed). The vehicle’s VIN is also displayed on the registration certificate and insurance policy documentation.

How much does it cost?

A Grumpy Cars report costs $20. This payment can be processed through PayPal and the report will be delivered almost instantly.

Why should I get a car history report?

The Grumpy Cars Report is a comprehensive documentation on the history of a used motor vehicle that has been registered or serviced. It provides information about the vehicle's history that may not be evident by looking at a vehicle in person. This helps people buy better and safer cars.
